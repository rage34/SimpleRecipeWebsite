Banana Pancakes

Start your morning off right with a stack of delicious and wholesome banana pancakes. These pancakes are not only a delight to the taste buds but also a nutritious way to kickstart your day. Made with ripe bananas and a few simple pantry staples, these pancakes are easy to whip up and perfect for satisfying those breakfast cravings.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Sweet breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Mash 1 ripe banana in a bowl until smooth. Combine the mashed banana with 1 cup flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, and 1/4 tsp salt. Pour in 3/4 cup milk, 1 tbsp melted butter or oil, and 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional). Mix until just combined.

step 2

Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

step 3

Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate. Serve warm, topped with sliced bananas, a drizzle of maple syrup, and a sprinkle of chopped nuts.

ingredients

1Ripe bananas

All-purpose flour

Baking powder

1cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
